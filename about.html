My story

Real insights should be
available to everyone.

Most nonprofits and small businesses are sitting on data that could completely change how they make decisions — but they can't afford an agency, and the tools are too complex to figure out alone.

I built Luma Analytics because I wanted to work on real problems, not textbook exercises. I take on projects across customer analytics, churn prediction, ad performance, and revenue forecasting — anything where the numbers aren't telling a clear story yet.

Every project is free. You get a working prediction model and a clear set of actions. I get real-world experience. No fees, no lock-in, no strings.
